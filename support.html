Common questions

How do I save a link to Klio?

Open Klio, tap the + button at the bottom, paste any URL, and tap Save. Klio will automatically generate a summary and tags for you.

How does semantic search work?

Type any idea, concept, or half-remembered sentence in the search bar. Klio searches by meaning, not just keywords, so you can find content even if you don't remember the exact words.

How do I generate AI collections?

Go to the Collections tab and tap Generate. Klio will analyze your library and create collections based on recurring themes across your saved content. This feature requires Klio Pro.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Find Klio Pro and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile in Klio, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
