Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Klio is built on a simple principle: your knowledge is yours. We collect only what is strictly necessary to provide the service, and we never sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Klio is a personal knowledge library application developed and operated by Stefano Palumbo ("we", "us", or "our"). You can reach us at hello@klio.space.

By using Klio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address — used for authentication and account management
  • Password — stored in encrypted form; we never have access to your plain-text password
  • Display name, username, and optional bio — provided by you in your profile
  • Profile photo — uploaded voluntarily by you

Content You Save

  • URLs you submit to be saved
  • Extracted content: titles, descriptions, thumbnails, transcripts (where available)
  • AI-generated summaries and tags created from the content you save
  • Vector embeddings generated for semantic search purposes
  • Collections you create and their associated items

Usage Data

  • Device language settings — used to display the app and generate AI content in your preferred language
  • Basic usage patterns necessary for app functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Klio service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Generate AI-powered summaries, tags, and semantic search capabilities
  • Display your saved content and collections within the app
  • Enable public collections you choose to share with other users
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send essential service communications (account-related only)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

4. Content Extraction from Third-Party Sources

Klio extracts content from URLs you explicitly provide. This includes web articles, YouTube videos, Substack newsletters, and other web content. This extraction occurs exclusively on your explicit request — Klio does not perform automated or unsolicited scraping.

Content extracted from third-party sources is:

  • Stored privately in your personal account
  • Used only to provide you with summaries, tags, and search functionality
  • Never shared with other users unless you explicitly choose to make a collection public

We use the following services for content extraction:

  • YouTube Data API v3 — for YouTube video metadata and transcripts, subject to Google API Terms of Service
  • Jina AI — for web article extraction

5. Third-Party Services

Klio relies on the following third-party providers to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI-generated summaries, tags, and embeddings. Content you save may be processed by OpenAI's API. Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Data API v3 — video metadata. Google Privacy Policy
  • Jina AI — web content extraction. Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Secure file storage for profile photos and collection covers

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Public Collections

Klio allows you to make collections public so other users can discover and follow them. When you make a collection public:

  • The collection name, description, and associated content become visible to all Klio users
  • Your username and display name are shown as the collection author
  • You can revert a collection to private at any time

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can:

  • Delete individual saved items at any time from within the app
  • Delete collections at any time from within the app
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us at hello@klio.space

Upon account deletion, all your personal data, saved content, and collections will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Klio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@klio.space.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@klio.space.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@klio.space
  • Website: klio.space
